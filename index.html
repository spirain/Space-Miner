Space Miner

You are Commander Alex Vega, a space miner sent to gather Stardust from deep space. Everything was going smoothly... until a strange cosmic storm threw your ship way off course.

Now you’re lost, far from Earth, with most of your systems offline. You only have a few minutes of life support left. Your only hope? Mine enough Stardust to power up your ship’s hyperdrive and escape.

Use your tools, gather Stardust fast, and upgrade your gear. The faster you click and the more you build, the better your chances.

Can you get back to Earth before time runs out? Or will you be trapped in space forever?

SELECT YOUR DIFFICULTY

Congratulations! You made it back to Earth!

As the final stardust particles surged into the hyperdrive core, your ship hummed to life. Lights flickered, the engines roared, and the navigation system locked onto Earth. You done it — against all odds.

The jump was violent. Stars twisted and bent, space folded, and then... calm. You emerged in Earth's orbit, battered but alive. Mission Control scrambled to respond. "Vega, is that you?!"

You had mined the impossible, survived the unthinkable, and returned home a legend. The universe tried to break you — and failed.

YOU LOSE! You ran out of oxygen!

The mining systems sputtered. Your oxygen reserves dipped into the red. With every passing second, the cold grip of space drew closer. You gave everything to gather enough Stardust... but time ran out.

In the silence of deep space, your ship drifted — a grave marker for a miner who dared to defy the cosmos. Earth never received your final transmission.

But legends aren't only written by those who return. Somewhere in the galaxy, a lonely distress beacon still pulses, telling the story of Commander Vega — the one who nearly escaped.
